POP Homes

installation, web design, poster design, printing, movel building, performance, design

POP—Practically Original Productions—Homes is a residential construction and real-estate company dedicated to the beautification of the suburban landscape and the empowerment of its residents. I created this project for my MFA Thesis in 2013. I created all the home models by using laser engraved/etched book board. There are 11 homes in all. I also created a series of poster advertising the company as well as a website for potential customers.
